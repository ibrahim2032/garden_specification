Objective/Goals

The objective of this mini site is to encourage visitors to take up gardening as a hobby and provide them with the necessary information and tools to help them get started. The site aims to promote physical activity, intellectual thinking, and well-being through gardening.

Key Audience

The target audience for this mini site is beginners who are interested in gardening or wish to learn how to garden. The site aims to cater to individuals of all ages who wish to start gardening but do not know where to begin. Additionally, the site aims to cater to individuals who have limited outdoor space but wish to grow their own herbs or vegetables.

Provisional Site Structure

  • Home Page - This page will contain an overview of the site, highlighting the benefits of gardening and providing links to other pages on the site.
  • Getting Started - This page will provide visitors with information on how to get started with gardening, including selecting the right plants for their space, soil preparation, and tools needed.
  • Growing Guide - This page will provide visitors with information on how to care for their plants and vegetables, including watering, fertilizing, and pest control.
  • Harvesting and Cooking - This page will provide visitors with information on how to harvest their produce and provide them with simple and easy-to-follow recipes.
  • Community Garden - This page will encourage visitors to get involved with community gardening projects and provide them with information on how to find a community garden in their local area.
  • Contact Form - This page will allow visitors to get in touch with the site owners and provide feedback or ask questions.

Functional Specification

  • Responsive design - The site will be designed to be responsive to ensure that it looks great on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • Content management system - The site will be built using a content management system (CMS) to enable easy updates and changes to the site's content.
  • Image gallery - The site will include an image gallery to showcase different types of plants and vegetables, helping visitors to identify the different types of plants they can grow.
  • Social media integration - The site will include social media integration, allowing visitors to share the site's content on their social media platforms.
  • Newsletter subscription - The site will include an option for visitors to subscribe to a newsletter to receive gardening tips and updates on the site's content.
